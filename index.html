What Tracecycle Does

Verified Material Data

Digital chain-of-custody from collection to processing. Moisture, contamination, CoA, sensor data — validated and timestamped.

Quality & Acceptance Workflow

Shipment creation, pre-checks, quality scoring, acceptance, rejection and certification — all in one consistent workflow.

Audit-Ready Reporting

Automated reporting for recyclers, WM operators, and certification bodies (ISCC+, RecyClass). Immutable history for compliance.
